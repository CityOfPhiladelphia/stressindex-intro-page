This application was developed jointly by the City of Philadelphia's Mayor's Office of Education (MOE) and the Office of Innovation and Technology's CityGeo team to inform the Community Schools Initiative.

 

By combining stressors including poverty, health, education, and employment, the Stress Index is used to pinpoint areas that would benefit most from community schools.

 

Community schools act as neighborhood resource centers for school-age children through older adults by facilitating access to programs  and services to help combat the steep barriers to educational achievement.
